Methodology

What the engine evaluates

Skill coverage

Checks how many of the role's core skills and tools are present in the resume, benchmarked per job title.

Contextual relevance

Compares overall document content against the role, not just isolated keyword hits.

Role benchmarking

Scores against curated skill profiles for common roles — from full-stack development to data science.

Instant verdict

Returns a clear strong / moderate / not-suitable rating so decisions don't wait on manual review.
